## Tips

1. To use an emoji, wrap the name in colons: `:smile:` ‚Üí :smile:
2. To create a footnote reference, use `[^identifier]`
3. To define a footnote, use `[^identifier]: Your footnote text`
4. Footnotes appear at the bottom of the document automatically
5. You can click on footnote references to jump to the definition and back

Enjoy using emojis :tada: and footnotes[^enjoy]!
